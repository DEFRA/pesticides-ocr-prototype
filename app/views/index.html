Register as a user of Plant Protection Products (PPPs)

Use this service to register yourself or your business if you work with, sell, or place plant protection products (PPPs) on the market in Great Britain.

Who must register

You must use this service if you:

  • use professional PPPs or adjuvants as part of your work (such as in agriculture, horticulture, forestry, or amenities)
  • place professional PPPs on the market, including manufacturers, importers, distributors, and corporate sellers
  • sell or supply amateur PPPs to the public (such as home and garden weedkillers, insecticides, or slug pellets)

Before you start

To complete this registration, you will need:

  • your business registration details including the address and details of any premises where PPPs are stored or sold
  • an estimate of the total quantities you store, sell, or use annually

If you operate across multiple sites

You only need to register your business once, but you will need to list the addresses of all permanent storage and retail sites during this application.

How long it takes

It takes around 10 to 15 minutes to complete this registration. You must finish the application in one session as your progress will not be saved automatically.
